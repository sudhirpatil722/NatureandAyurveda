Sprained Ankle

Sprain is a common phenomenon observed due to joint twists, thereby leading to ligament injury. Ligaments connect the bones in the joint and therefore should be intact to ensure healthy bones.

Ankle sprains are generally caused by running or walking on uneven ground, donning high-heeled footwear or lifting heavy weights.

Pain, swelling, bruising and restricted range of motion are common symptoms.
