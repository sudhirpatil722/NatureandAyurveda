Polycystic Ovary Syndrome

Polycystic ovary syndrome is a common condition related to a hormonal imbalance in women of reproductive age. It is characterized by high levels of androgens in the body.

The condition leads to issues like changes in the menstrual cycle, cysts on the ovaries and problems getting pregnant.

Although there is no cure for this problem yet, it can be controlled. Some simple home-made remedies can help manage the symptoms and reduce complications.
