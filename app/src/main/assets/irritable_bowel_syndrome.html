Irritable Bowel Syndrome (IBS)

Irritable bowel syndrome is a symptom-based diagnosis characterized by chronic abdominal pain, discomfort, bloating and alteration of bowel habits.

Some of the most common symptoms of IBS are abdominal cramping or pain, diarrhoea, constipation, bloating, gas, mucus in the stool, nausea, headache, depression and fatigue.
